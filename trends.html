Discovery Insights
Key findings from the 14-phase investigation
Phase 2 (Title×Language search) and Phase 6 (Full Classify) yielded 44.7% of all domains. These two phases alone accounted for 3,230 of the 7,225 total domains discovered, demonstrating the effectiveness of combining title-based search with comprehensive classification.
Diminishing returns after Phase 11. The final three phases (12–14) contributed only 772 domains (10.7%), suggesting the investigation has achieved >90% coverage of surface-web game piracy infrastructure.
Severity 4 dominates. Over half of all domains (52.4%) carry a severity score of 4, indicating widespread high-impact infringement across the ecosystem.
English-language sites represent the majority. 3,586 domains (82.5%) primarily serve English-language content, followed by Russian (375) and Japanese (155), reflecting global distribution patterns.
