Analysis & Insights
Key findings from trend decomposition
Streaming dominance accelerating. Streaming ecotype share grew from 12% to 34% over 7 quarters, overtaking both Torrent (-15pp) and DDL (-13pp). This shift correlates with improved video CDN availability and lower hosting costs in Southeast Asia.
Seasonal spikes align with content release cycles. January (index 1.28) and August (1.22) consistently show peak piracy activity, coinciding with winter anime season launches and summer blockbuster releases respectively. June dips to 0.81, the annual trough.
DRM arms race shows diminishing piracy returns. Denuvo v7 extends mean crack time to 96 days, a 5.2x improvement over v5. However, 42% of Denuvo-protected titles were still cracked within the critical 30-day sales window, suggesting DRM alone is insufficient.
Domain growth remains structurally positive. Net domain growth has averaged +37/month over the trailing 12 months. Takedowns are accelerating (+18% YoY) but new registrations outpace removals by a 1.4:1 ratio. The ecosystem is expanding despite enforcement pressure.
